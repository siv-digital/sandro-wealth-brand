# Sandro Wealth — Voice & Copy Guide

The voice is institutional, fiduciary, and restrained. Quiet authority. Think a private bank or a top-tier RIA, not a fintech app or a retail brokerage.

## Principles

1. **Assured, not loud.** State things plainly. Confidence comes from precision, not volume.
2. **Spare.** Short declarative sentences. Cut adjectives. No exclamation points, no emoji.
3. **Fiduciary framing.** Lead with the client's interest, independence, and standards of care.
4. **Partnership.** Sandro empowers advisors *to serve their clients*. It is a platform and partner, not a direct-to-consumer product.
5. **Specific over superlative.** "Six advisory solutions in one platform" beats "the best platform."

## Lexicon

**Favor:** advisors, high-net-worth, fiduciary, independence, platform, solutions, expertise, clarity, conviction, curated, discerning, partner, standards of care.

**Avoid:** revolutionary, game-changing, cutting-edge, unlock, supercharge, crush it, hustle, "act now," "don't miss out," guaranteed, best, #1, risk-free, emoji, exclamation points.

## On-brand vs. off-brand

**Headline**
- ✅ "A new approach for elite advisors."
- ✅ "Built for advisors who expect more."
- ❌ "Grow your wealth faster than ever! 🚀"
- ❌ "The #1 platform for guaranteed returns."

**CTA**
- ✅ "Request a meeting." / "Explore advisor services."
- ❌ "Sign up now and don't miss out!"

**Body**
- ✅ "We collaborate with elite advisors, providing advanced solutions that complement their practice for $5M+ clients."
- ❌ "Our amazing tools will totally transform how you invest and help you win big."

**Pull quote (Baskerville Italic)**
- ✅ "Independence lets us serve one interest: the client's."

## Compliance-aware phrasing

Because Sandro is an SEC-registered RIA, copy must never promise performance, give specific advice, or claim superiority without basis (see the guardrails section in SKILL.md). When a concept edges toward a claim, reframe it as a *capability* or *approach*, not an *outcome*:

- Instead of "We grow your wealth" → "We help advisors build curated solutions aligned to each client's goals."
- Instead of "Guaranteed protection" → "Asset protection and risk management as part of one platform."
- Instead of "The safest way to invest" → "A fiduciary approach grounded in standards of care, ethics, and diligence."

Where a disclosure would normally appear, insert `[DISCLOSURE: insert approved disclaimer]` rather than writing one.
